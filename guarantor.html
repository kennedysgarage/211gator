Guarantor

In addition to completing a credit check, a guarantor needs to provide the following documents:

  • Two most-recent pay stubs
  • Most-recent tax return
  • A letter from his or her employer confirming employment and salary
  • A copy of current Drivers License or Approved Government picture ID
  • Monthly income totals at least three times monthly rent
