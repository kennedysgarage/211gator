Guarantor

In addition to completing a credit check, a guarantor needs to provide the following documents:

  • A copy of current Drivers License or Approved Government picture ID
  • Monthly income totals at least three times monthly rent
  • Two most-recent pay stubs or most-recent tax return or a letter from his or her employer confirming employment and salary

General, Unconditional and Continuing Guaranty

The guarantor should download the General, Unconditional and Continuing Guaranty form, fill it out, and send it back to us.

Credit Check

The guarantor should fill out the credit check form. Each credit check is $19.95 and non-refundable.
